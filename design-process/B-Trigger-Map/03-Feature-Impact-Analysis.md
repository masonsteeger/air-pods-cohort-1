## MVP Recommendation

**Core MVP (Must-Have - Score 16+):** Features 1-4 — a single, stable-URL page that auto-syncs from Dawn's sheet, lists all events with Meetup links. These features directly serve Pete (external prospects) and Oscar (external organizers), the primary drivers of external relationship building.

**Enhanced MVP (Should-Have - Score 11-15):** Add cancellation visibility, group details, date sorting, share links, and topic tags for a polished launch experience. These features improve usability and amplify Oscar's ability to promote groups, strengthening the external relationship strategy.

**Strategic Note:** The reprioritization elevates Oscar (external organizer) to Priority 2, recognizing his role as an amplifier who brings more Petes into Improving's network. Features that serve Oscar now score higher, reflecting the focus on external community connections over internal convenience.
